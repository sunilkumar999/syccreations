Who We Are

Syc Creations is a digital design creative group within Student Media. Syc Creations provides fee-based services to clients on the campus of ISU as well as throughout the greater Wabash Valley.

From video production, to graphic design, to web design and development, Syc Creations seeks to be a one-stop shop for your digital creative needs.

Students working for Syc Creations came from diverse majors, including computer science, graphic design, communication, and business.

Our Mission

Our mission of Syc Creations is to provide our clients with precise, clean, skillful, and creative digital products in a timely manner.

Web Design • Web Development • Graphic Design
Motion Graphics • Video Production
Strategic Communication • Social Media
